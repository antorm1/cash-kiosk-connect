# Cash Kiosk Connect

Cash-to-digital onramp for immigrant-heavy commerce.

## What this does
- Helps merchants serve cash-first customers with digital payments
- Covers local languages, assisted onboarding, and offline fallback
- Targets neighborhoods with high remittance and cash circulation

## Why now
Global digital finance still assumes bank accounts, cards, and native-level digital access. Large populations remain fully outside that loop.
